Daring Greatly

Description: "Daring Greatly" is a self-help and personal development book that explores the concept of vulnerability and its transformative power. Brené Brown, a research professor at the University of Houston, draws on her research and experiences to discuss how embracing vulnerability can lead to greater courage, creativity, and connection in various aspects of life. The book examines the impact of shame, fear, and vulnerability in relationships, parenting, leadership, and personal growth, and it offers practical guidance on how to cultivate resilience and wholehearted living.

Popular Line in Book: "Vulnerability is not winning or losing; it's having the courage to show up and be seen when we have no control over the outcome."

Genre: Self-Help, Personal Development, Psychology, Non-Fiction

Publication Date:"Daring Greatly" was first published on September 11, 2012.

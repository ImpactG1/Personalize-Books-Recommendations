Description: "Educated" is a memoir that tells the remarkable true story of Tara Westover's journey from a rural and isolated upbringing in rural Idaho, where she had no formal education, to eventually earning a Ph.D. from Cambridge University. Tara's childhood was marked by extreme circumstances, including a lack of formal schooling, but her determination and thirst for knowledge led her to seek education against all odds. The book explores themes of family, resilience, the power of education, and the quest for self-discovery.

Popular Line in Book:"You can love someone and still choose to say goodbye to them," which reflects the complex relationships within Tara's family as she pursued education and personal growth.

Genre: Memoir, Autobiography, Non-Fiction

Publication Date: "Educated" was first published on February 20, 2018.

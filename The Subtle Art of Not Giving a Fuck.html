The Subtle Art of Not Giving a Fuck

Description: "The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck" is a self-help and personal development book that challenges conventional wisdom about happiness and success. Mark Manson argues that the pursuit of constant positivity and avoiding discomfort can lead to unhappiness. Instead, he advocates for embracing life's struggles and choosing what to care about. The book combines humor, candid storytelling, and practical advice to help readers prioritize what truly matters and lead a more meaningful life.

Popular Line in Book: A popular line from the book is: "In life, we have a limited amount of fcks to give. So you must choose your fcks wisely."

Ego Is The Enemy

Description: "Ego Is the Enemy" is a book that explores the detrimental impact of ego on personal and professional growth. Ryan Holiday delves into historical and contemporary examples to demonstrate how ego can hinder success and offers guidance on how to overcome it. The book emphasizes the importance of humility, self-awareness, and continuous self-improvement.

Popular Line in Book: "The only way to be comfortable with death is to understand and see yourself as something bigger than yourself"

Genre: Self-help, Personal Development, Psychology and Stoicism.

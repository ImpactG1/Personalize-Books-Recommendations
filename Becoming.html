Description: "Becoming" is a memoir by former First Lady of the United States, Michelle Obama. In the book, Michelle Obama shares her personal journey, from her childhood in Chicago to her experiences as a lawyer, mother, and ultimately, the First Lady of the United States during Barack Obama's presidency. She reflects on her upbringing, her family, her time in the White House, and her advocacy work on issues such as education and health. The book is a candid and inspiring account of her life and the values that have guided her.

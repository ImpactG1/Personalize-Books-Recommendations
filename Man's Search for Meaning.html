Man's Search for Meaning

Description: "Man's Search for Meaning" is a profound and influential book that explores the author's experiences as a Holocaust survivor and psychiatrist. Viktor Frankl reflects on his time in Nazi concentration camps and the mental and emotional struggles faced by inmates. He introduces the concept of logotherapy, a psychotherapeutic approach centered on finding meaning in life, even in the most challenging circumstances. The book not only offers a powerful account of survival but also provides insights into the human capacity for resilience and the search for purpose and meaning.

The Power of Habit: Why We Do What We Do in Life and Business

Description: "Why We Do What We Do in Life and Business" explores the science of habit formation and the role of habits in our personal and professional lives. Charles Duhigg delves into the neurological and psychological mechanisms that underlie habits and how they can be harnessed or changed to achieve personal and organizational goals.

Popular Line in Book: "Habits, scientists say, emerge because the brain is constantly looking for ways to save effort."

Genre: Psychology, Self-Help, Business, Non-Fiction

Publication Date: "Why We Do What We Do in Life and Business" was first published on February 28, 2012.

Born a Crime

Description: "Born a Crime" is a memoir by comedian and television host Trevor Noah. The book tells the story of his childhood growing up in South Africa during the final years of apartheid and the early years of post-apartheid South Africa. Trevor Noah recounts his experiences as the son of a white Swiss father and a black Xhosa mother, which made him "born a crime" under apartheid's racial laws. The book is a humorous and insightful exploration of his upbringing, family, and the challenges of navigating a racially divided society.

Popular Line in Book: "I didn't have an easy life, but I had a simple one."

Genre: Memoir, Autobiography, Humor, Non-Fiction

Publication Date: "Born a Crime" was first published on November 15, 2016.

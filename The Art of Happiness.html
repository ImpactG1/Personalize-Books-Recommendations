Dalai Lama (Tenzin Gyatso) and Howard Cutler

The Art of Happiness

Description: "The Art of Happiness" is a book that explores the concept of happiness and how individuals can achieve lasting happiness and well-being. The book is based on a series of conversations and interviews between the Dalai Lama, the spiritual leader of Tibetan Buddhism, and Dr. Howard Cutler, a psychiatrist. Together, they discuss the principles of happiness, the nature of the mind, and practical strategies for leading a more joyful and fulfilling life. The book combines Eastern philosophy with Western psychology to offer insights into finding happiness in everyday life.

Popular Line in Book: "The purpose of our lives is to be happy."

Genre: Self-Help, Philosophy, Spiritual, Non-Fiction

Publication Date: "The Art of Happiness" was first published in 1998.

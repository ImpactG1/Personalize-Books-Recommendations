Atomic Habits

Description: "Atomic Habits" is a self-help and personal development book that explores the science of habit formation and provides practical strategies for building positive habits and breaking negative ones. James Clear explains how small, incremental changes, or "atomic habits," can lead to significant personal transformation over time. He delves into the psychology of habits, the role of environment, and the importance of habit stacking and accountability in achieving lasting change.

Popular Line in Book: One of the popular lines from the book is: "You do not rise to the level of your goals. You fall to the level of your systems."

Genre: Self-Help, Personal Development, Psychology

Publication Date: "Atomic Habits" was first published on October 16, 2018.

Power of money

Description: "The Power of Money" is a book that explores the history and evolution of ideas about money and their profound impact on the modern world. Robert Pringle delves into the history of money, banking, and finance, tracing the development of economic thought and the role of money in shaping economies and societies. The book examines the influence of key economic thinkers and theories on monetary policy, banking systems, and the global financial landscape.

Popular Line in Book: “Money is power, freedom, a cushion, the root of all evil, the sum of blessings.”

Genre: Non-Fiction, Economics, Finance, History

Publication Date: "The Power of Money: How Ideas about Money Shaped the Modern World" was published on June 28, 2014.

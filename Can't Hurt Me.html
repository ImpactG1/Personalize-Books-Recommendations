Can't Hurt Me

Description: "Can't Hurt Me" is a memoir and self-help book by David Goggins, a former Navy SEAL and ultramarathon runner. The book chronicles his extraordinary journey from a troubled childhood marked by poverty and abuse to becoming one of the world's toughest endurance athletes. David Goggins shares his experiences, struggles, and the mental and physical challenges he overcame to achieve his goals. He also provides insights into developing mental toughness, resilience, and the ability to push through adversity.

Popular Line in Book: "Motivation is crap. Motivation comes and goes. When you're driven, whatever is in front of you will get destroyed."

Genre: Memoir, Self-Help, Non-Fiction

Publication Date: "Can't Hurt Me" was first published on November 15, 2018.

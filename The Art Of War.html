The Art Of War

Description: "The Art of War" is a classic Chinese text on strategy, tactics, and warfare. It is often considered one of the most influential works on military strategy and has applications beyond the battlefield. The book provides insights into the principles of leadership, tactics, and strategy, which can be applied to various aspects of life and business.

Popular Line in Book: "All warfare is based on deception."

Genre: Business, Leadership, Strategy and Tactics

Publication Date: It is believed to have been written during the Warring States period of ancient China, between the 5th and 3rd centuries BCE.

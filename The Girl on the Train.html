The Girl on the Train

Description: "The Girl on the Train" is a psychological thriller novel that tells the story of three women whose lives become intertwined in a complex and suspenseful narrative. The book follows Rachel, a troubled woman who becomes obsessed with observing a couple from her daily train commute. When she believes she witnesses a shocking event, she becomes entangled in a mystery involving deceit, betrayal, and hidden truths. The novel explores themes of memory, perception, and the unreliability of narrators.

Popular Line in Book: "You don't know her. But she knows you."

Genre: Psychological Thriller, Mystery, Fiction

Publication Date: "The Girl on the Train" was first published on January 13, 2015.

Bhagvat Gita

Description: The Bhagavad Gita is a sacred text that addresses profound philosophical and spiritual concepts. It offers guidance on ethics, duty, and the nature of the self. The core message of the Gita revolves around the concept of dharma (duty) and the paths to spiritual realization.

Popular Line in Book: "You have the right to perform your prescribed duties, but you are not entitled to the fruits of your actions."

Genre: Philosophical and Spiritual

Publication Date: It is believed to have been composed between the 5th and 2nd centuries BCE.
